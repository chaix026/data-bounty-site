**Abstract:**
Nitrogen fertilizer overuse is a significant contributor to water pollution. This study investigates whether behavioral "nudges"—specifically, peer comparison feedback and personalized recommendations—can encourage farmers to optimize their fertilizer usage without compromising yield.
