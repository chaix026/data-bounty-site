**Introduction:**
When randomized controlled trials (RCTs) are not feasible, data scientists must rely on quasi-experimental methods. This post compares two popular approaches: Synthetic Control Methods (SCM) and Time-Based Regression (TBR), discussing their assumptions, strengths, and use cases in marketing measurement.
